# Project Brief

## Purpose

Build Nordic Wilds as a Hades-style isometric roguelite with two linked campaigns: a Nordic first act and a medieval Japan second act. The game should emphasize fast combat, strong atmosphere, room-clear encounters, and a visual identity that changes clearly between eras.

## Target Users

- Players who like action roguelites with responsive combat and short replayable runs.
- Players who enjoy mythology-inspired settings and strong environmental art direction.
- Players who want a game that mixes kinetic combat with light progression and exploration.

## Core Experience

- Enter a room.
- Fight a wave or set of enemies.
- Choose a reward or path.
- Move deeper into the biome.
- Unlock or transition into the next era.

## Success Criteria

- Combat feels responsive in the first minute.
- The Nordic and Japan sections are visually distinct.
- The player can understand room state, enemy danger, and reward state at a glance.
- The project can support future bosses, weapons, boons, and story beats without large rewrites.
